Welcome to the url-shortener service.

Enter short_array index to go to site

Example: https://floating-headland-24202.herokuapp.com/12

Enter /list to list database

Example: https://floating-headland-24202.herokuapp.com/list

Enter /new/url to shorten new url

Example: https://floating-headland-24202.herokuapp.com/new/http://www.example.com
